About

My name is Grey. I am a highschool student from the United States. For as long as I can remember, I have loved to create things. When I was in the fifth grade, my teacher introduced me to a programming website called Scratch. I found enjoyment in making little projects with this website. After about a year of making Scratch projects, I moved on to Python, then JavaScript and more recently Rust. Over this time, I have also spread out from programming into other creative skills such as digital art, sewing and music. I have created this website to showcase some of these projects.
